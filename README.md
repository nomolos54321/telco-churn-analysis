# Customer Churn Analysis (Python)

---

## Overview

This project analyses telecom customer data to identify key factors driving customer churn and provide actionable business recommendations.

The analysis was conducted using Python, focusing on data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and visualisation.

---

## Tools Used

- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Seaborn & Matplotlib

---

## Key Questions

- What is the overall churn rate?
- Does contract type influence churn?
- Does internet service type affect churn?
- Are higher-paying customers more likely to churn?

---

## Key Findings

- Approximately **26.6% of customers churned**
- Customers on **month-to-month contracts** had the highest churn
- **Fiber optic customers** showed higher churn compared to DSL users
- Customers with **higher monthly charges** were more likely to churn

---

## Key Insight

Customers most likely to churn are:

- On **month-to-month contracts**
- Using **fiber optic internet**
- Paying **higher monthly charges**

This suggests that customers with low commitment and higher costs are at the greatest risk of leaving.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to move to longer-term contracts
- Review pricing and value for fiber optic services
- Target high-risk customers with retention offers
- Improve value perception for higher-paying customers
